SideFill Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

The short version: SideFill keeps your data in your iCloud account, not ours. We don't run servers that store your data, and we don't track you.

What SideFill Does

SideFill is a tool for live-sound professionals to manage input lists, stage plots, and changeover notes for shows and festivals. You enter the data, SideFill stores it, and your crew can see it.

Where Your Data Lives

The data you create in SideFill — events, input lists, stage plots, notes, attached files — is stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework. Specifically:

  • Your data lives in a private CloudKit database tied to your Apple ID.
  • HogBody Labs does not operate any servers that store your SideFill data.
  • HogBody Labs cannot read, access, or recover your SideFill data.
  • Your data syncs between your own devices (iPhone, iPad, Mac) signed into the same Apple ID.

Apple's privacy practices apply to data stored in iCloud. You can review Apple's iCloud privacy information at apple.com/legal/privacy.

Sharing Events with Your Crew

SideFill lets you share an event with other people (your crew). When you share an event:

  • Apple's CloudKit Sharing (CKShare) handles the share invitation and access.
  • The crew member you invite can see the event data you've shared with them.
  • You control who has access. You can revoke access at any time.
  • Shared data is still stored in iCloud, not on HogBody Labs servers.

If a crew member you've shared an event with chooses to leave the share, their access is removed.

What Apple Sees

Because SideFill uses Apple's CloudKit, Apple processes your data on our behalf to provide sync and sharing. Apple's handling of CloudKit data is described in Apple's iCloud privacy disclosures.

Push Notifications

SideFill registers for Apple Push Notifications (APNs) only to receive silent CloudKit sync notifications. These keep your data up to date across your devices and your crew's devices. SideFill does not send marketing or promotional push notifications.

No Analytics or Tracking

SideFill contains:

  • No analytics SDKs (no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude, none)
  • No crash reporting services that send data off-device
  • No advertising frameworks
  • No fingerprinting or device identification
  • No tracking pixels, beacons, or telemetry

No Accounts

SideFill does not create or require a HogBody Labs account. The only sign-in is your existing Apple ID, which Apple handles. We never see your password, and we don't collect your email address.

No Ads

SideFill does not display advertisements and never will.

Children's Privacy

SideFill does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Because SideFill doesn't collect data centrally, this is largely moot.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your SideFill data lives in your iCloud account. To delete it:

  • Delete events, input lists, or other items inside the app.
  • Or delete the SideFill app, which removes its local copy.
  • To remove all SideFill data from iCloud, sign into iCloud Drive on a Mac, find the SideFill data, and delete it — or contact Apple support.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we'll change the date at the top. Material changes will be reflected in this document.
