RealMPH Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

The short version: RealMPH stays on your device. We don't collect your location, your speed, or anything else.

What RealMPH Does

RealMPH is a GPS speedometer for iPhone and CarPlay. It reads your device's GPS data to show your current speed and the maximum speed reached since you opened the app.

Location Data

RealMPH requests access to your location for one reason: to calculate your speed from GPS. Your location is:

  • Used only on your device to compute speed
  • Never sent to HogBody Labs
  • Never sent to any server, anywhere
  • Never stored on disk — speed is computed in real time and discarded
  • Never shared with anyone

What's Stored on Your Device

RealMPH does not persist your data between sessions. The maximum speed shown during a session is held in memory and reset to zero when you close the app. There is no trip log, no history, and no usage data saved.

No Accounts

RealMPH does not require or offer account creation. There is no sign-up, no login, and no email collection.

No Analytics or Tracking

RealMPH contains:

  • No analytics SDKs
  • No crash reporting services
  • No advertising frameworks
  • No fingerprinting or device identification
  • No tracking pixels, beacons, or telemetry

No Ads

RealMPH does not display advertisements and never will.

Children's Privacy

RealMPH does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not collect information from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we'll change the date at the top. Since RealMPH doesn't collect data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
